# FundiPlug

> **Connecting Skilled Professionals with Opportunity**

FundiPlug is a secure and scalable digital marketplace that connects customers with verified service providers across multiple trades and professional service categories. The platform simplifies service discovery, hiring, communication, and business growth through a trusted ecosystem built for both individuals and organizations.

---

# Vision

To become Africa's leading digital marketplace for skilled professionals by empowering service providers, businesses, and customers through technology.

---

# Problem Statement

Finding reliable service providers remains a challenge for many individuals and businesses. Customers often face:

* Difficulty finding trusted professionals
* Limited visibility into service quality
* Unclear pricing and accountability
* Fragmented hiring and communication processes

Service providers often struggle with:

* Limited access to clients
* Lack of online visibility
* Inconsistent job opportunities
* Difficulty building trust and credibility

FundiPlug bridges this gap by creating a secure, transparent, and efficient marketplace for service delivery.

# Marketplace Workflow

1. Customer posts a job or searches for a service.
2. Verified fundis and businesses respond.
3. Customer reviews profiles and proposals.
4. Hiring is completed through the platform.
5. Service is delivered.
6. Reviews and ratings are submitted.
7. Trust and accountability are maintained through verification and monitoring.

# Security Architecture

Security is a foundational pillar of FundiPlug.

### Authentication & Access Control

* Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
* Row Level Security (RLS)
* Account lockout protection
* Refresh token rotation
* Token revocation and blacklist validation

### Data Protection

* Private storage buckets
* Secure file access controls
* Content validation and upload filtering
* Secure API authorization mechanisms

### Platform Security

* Hardened database access policies
* Secure CORS configurations
* Business verification workflows
* Audit-ready authentication controls
